## Key Findings Summary

### Data Preprocessing
- **Initial Data**: 415 samples (313 ALS, 102 Control)
- **Final Dataset**: 4,472 SNVs across 1,247 miRNAs
- **Key Transformations**: G>T filtering, mutation splitting, duplicate collapsing, VAF calculation, quality filtering

### Analytical Approaches
- **Positional Analysis**: Position 6 hotspot, seed region significance
- **Oxidative Load**: Controls show higher oxidative load than ALS (p < 0.001)
- **PCA Analysis**: Partial separation between groups after artifact exclusion
- **Clinical Correlations**: Age and sex significantly correlate with oxidative load
- **Pathway Analysis**: miR-181 family most contributive to differentiation

### Integration with Original Paper
- **miR-181 Focus**: 63 SNVs related to miR-181 family identified
- **Complementary Findings**: SNV patterns complement expression-based findings
- **Clinical Integration**: Our oxidative load scores correlate with clinical variables
- **Multi-omics Approach**: Comprehensive molecular understanding of ALS

### Comparison Framework
- **Methodological**: Complementary approaches (expression vs SNV patterns)
- **Biological**: Multi-level molecular changes in miR-181 family
- **Clinical**: Both expression and SNV patterns clinically relevant
- **Statistical**: Both approaches show high significance and robust validation
